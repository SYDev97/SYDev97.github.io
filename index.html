Proposed Project: Rental Property Managemet Platform

The proposed project will be a rental property management platform & company. The idea behind the company & platform would be to give home owners to tools & marketplace to rent out their properties over a longer period of time. The project would not only designed as a website but would also be designed to assist property owners throughout the entire process and even give the user access to dedicated teams of professionals to assist them.

In 2016, the Austrlian census shows that the percentage of Austrlians renting had increased to 30.9% (1) of the population and home owners make up around 60% (2) of the market.

Developing such a platform would enable home owners who have little to no experience renting out their properties to gain access to a second stream of income by using such a platform. Not only this, but having a such a platform widely available to the general public, it would allow these home owners to rent out their properties and spare rooms without having to pay up to 15% of their weekly rent (3) on management costs.

The property management platform would be deisgned in a way that would allow every day home owners who have no experience renting or manageing a property to do so.The platform would be designed to allow property owners to rent out their homes, apartment or spare rooms over longer periods of times, rather than by a single nightly rate like other similar platform. The platform itself as well as the company behind the platform would contain a number of different sections and features to make this easier.

As part of this platform, it would be designed to contain multiple section, a public property lising, a "renters" dashboard and a "managers" dashboard. With each section playing a role in how the entire platform operates different.

The first section, the public property listing, would be a place where any user (account or not) could browse through the properties available for rent on the platform. It would also indicate if the property was currently rented and when the property would become vacant again. As part of a safety and security measure, the platform would automatically hide the address of the prperty from the public listing when the property is being rented out.

The second section, would be the renters dashboard. As part of the renters dahsboard, they would be given access to tools that would allow them to easily contact the landord, manage a current rental, aply for another rental & even manage payments for their current rental. The dashboard for the renter would be designed in a way that allows them easy control and access to information about the property and the landlord as well as information and the remaining duration of their lease.

The final section of the platform would be the mamangers dashboard. As part of the manager dashbaord, the user would be given access to tools that would allow them to book inspections of their property to ensure the tennent is keeping it clean and tidy, manage applications for new renters, manage the current tenent, get in contact with current and future tennents of the propery and more. The manage dashboard would also allow the user to manageme multiple properties as well as manage the information that is on the webasite about their properties. This information would include images of the property, description, weekly or monthly rent as well as giving the user an option to mark their property as ready to be rented out or not.

Ideally this platform would be designed and developed using a Software as a Service model, meaning that the website would designed and developed as a web based. To have this completed successfully, the development team would require a basic knowledge of the MERN technolofy stack or some equivilent.

The MERN technology stack is a JavaScript stack centered around using MongoDB, the ExpressJS framework, React JS and Node.js as its core libraries. To ensure this style of software can run smoothly, the platfrom would have to hosted on a dedicated sevrer, a Virtual Private Server or some equivilent of a cloud hosting that supports NodeJS applications.

All other libraries and frameworks that would be required for such a platform, including; session management, cookies, payment processing are already open source.

If this project were to be successfull, it would not only disrupt the rental market in Australia, it could also be expanded upon to become an interanational platform and help disrupt the property rental market in other countries. Not only this, but if this platform were successful it would give property owners access to a passive income from their properties or spare rooms at home.
